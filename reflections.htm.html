    This last web page is meant to serve as a summary of my experiences and knowledge that I am taking away from this course. I went into this class with the goal of better understanding how to make a website in order to help my mom start up her business. It was also just for the fun of knowing how websites work in the first place. a few weeks into the class and realizing how easy it really is to type HTML I got hooked on it and had a new passion for website building. In the future I would like to turn this into a hobby just to test around my skills and maybe host my own website some day. I wish I had more time this quarter to learn more about CSS and XHTML and Java as I know those are key to making a good website but due to the nature of full time college this will have to wait. As time goes on I do plan to update this website of mine to further express the skill that I am continuing to learn on my own time. The biggest AHA! moment I had was actually rather minor to most people and that was how links to other websites or even the same pages as yours work. This opens up a world of possibilities to stay connected.
